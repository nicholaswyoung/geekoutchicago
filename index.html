Geek Out

Chicago

There are many gatherings in Chicago, the problem is - you don't know about most of them. There's the Geek Breakfast, Chicago DB, and LikeMind - just to name a few. In an effort to give their community a gift, many talented organizers put time (and some, money) into their event. It's time that we give them the recognition they deserve.

Geek Out Chicago's monthly mailing keeps you in the loop. What events are hot, why you should attend, and who will be there. Finally, one social calendar to rule them all.
